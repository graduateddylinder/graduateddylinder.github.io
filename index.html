I am a PhD student in Earth and Space Science at York University in Toronto, Canada My research focuses on measuring the electromagnetic properties of rocks and minerals in order to better understand radar imagery of planetary surfaces In particular, I use these measurements to develop models and estimate properties of Near Earth Asteroids such as 101955 Bennu, the target of NASA's OSIRIS-REx asteroid sample return mission
